THE RULES

2026 Legal Warnings

  • 🚫 Vaping: 100% Illegal. Possession = Arrest.
  • 🌿 Cannabis: Medical only. Needs prescription (available on-site). No public smoking.
  • 👑 Monarchy: Never joke/criticize the Royal Family. Very strict laws!

Temple Etiquette

  • 👗 Dress Code: Shoulders and knees MUST be covered. No tank tops.
  • 👟 Shoes: Remove before entering any shrine or house.
  • 🧘 Buddha: Never point your feet at Buddha statues.

Pro Tips

  • 🛵 Scooters: Don't ride without a license/helmet. Highly dangerous.
  • 🇹🇭 Anthem: Stand still if the National Anthem plays (8 AM/6 PM in public).
  • 💧 Hydration: Tap water IS NOT safe. Drink bottled only.
